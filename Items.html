Rogan Josh

Rogan josh is a curried meat dish originating from Kashmir. It is made with red meat, usually lamb, mutton, or goat, and coloured and flavoured by alkanet flower and Kashmiri chilies. It is one of the main dishes of the Kashmiri multicourse meal called wazwan. It was introduced to Kashmir by the Mughals, who were influenced by Persian cuisine.

Kothu Parotta

Kothu parotta is a Tamil Nadu street food where shredded flatbreads are cooked with salna (a spicy gravy) and other ingredients like eggs, vegetables or meat. It is a filling and flavorful dish that originated from Sri Lanka and is popular in South India and other countries. It is usually served with onion raita (a yogurt-based dip)

Chicken 65

Chicken 65 is a spicy, deep-fried chicken dish that originated from Hotel Buhari in Chennai, India. It is made with boneless chicken marinated with curd, chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder and other spices, and then fried until crisp and golden. It is usually served as an appetizer or snack with onion and lemon garnish.

Filter Coffee

Filter coffee is a coffee drink made by brewing finely ground coffee powder with hot water using a filter. The filter can be made of paper, metal or cloth and it separates the coffee grounds from the liquid. Filter coffee is popular in South India and some other parts of the world. It has a clear and strong flavor and can be mixed with milk and sugar.

Reuben Sandwich

A Reuben sandwich is a grilled sandwich made with corned beef, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Russian dressing or Thousand Island dressing on rye bread. It is a popular American dish that has several claims of origin, but is commonly associated with kosher-style delicatessens. It has a savory and tangy taste and can be served with chips or pickles.

Sourdough Bread

Sourdough bread is a type of leavened bread that is made by fermenting dough with wild yeast and lactic acid bacteria. These microbes give the bread a tangy flavor and help preserve it. Sourdough bread is one of the oldest forms of bread and may have originated in ancient Egypt.
